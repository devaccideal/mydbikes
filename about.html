About MYD Bicycle Rides

Enjoy environmental friendly bicycle rides with us

MYD BICYCLE RIDES established in 2015 moves forward until now providing the best for our clients who love to explore the countryside of Galle, Sri Lanka and the ones who love to ride bicycles. We have a crew of ten guiding the cyclists throughout the ride and who grant their protection and the needed facilities. Our team is aware of all the routes the cyclists follow in their riding trials. We also grant that our bicycle rides provide safety for the riders and as well as the environment.
