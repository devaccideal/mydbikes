MYD BICYCLE RIDES MIHIRIPENNA, GALLE, SRI LANKA

Why Us ?

Since 2015, MYD bicycle rides have been exploring the countryside of Galle. Our tours unveil the natural beauty of the Galle countryside. We provide you a rare chance to pass freely through the greenish paddy fields, tea plantations, and mangrove swamps. In addition, see the beauty of birds and animals endemic to Sri Lanka. We grant your safety in rides and provide you all equipment and facilities needed. Two designated guides for all trips will guide each tour.

OUR MISSION

Our bicycle rides exploring the brilliance and peacefulness of nature target to provide enjoyment and relaxation to our clients.
